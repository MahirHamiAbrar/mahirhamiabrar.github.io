About Me

I'm currently pursuing my B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering at RUET (Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology), where I focus on competitive programming, AI Applications, and Exploring new ideas outside my domain alongside my academic studies.

I've built AI tools, real-time IoT based systems, and experimented on various linux distros even implemented a very basic custom window manager.

I specialize in:

  • RAG-based Chat Systems (LangGraph, LLMs, vector DBs)
  • Embedded Systems & Robotics (Arduino + ESP32 + FreeRTOS + Computer Vision)
  • Desktop Applications (Python, C++, Qt6, custom GUIs on Linux/X11)
  • AI infrastructure, tools, assistants

Currently building: IntelliTube, an LLM-powered platform that summarizes YouTube contents, documents, webpages using LangChain, LangGraph, Groq, Ollama and vector databases (Qdrant).
